Escape Sequence charecters are special charecters in c 
which starts with backslash \ . 
These are non printable charecters used within strings or charecters.

\n - Newline
\t - Horizontal tab
\b - Backspace
\r - Carriage return
\' - Single quote '
\" - Double quote "
\\ - Backslash \
\0 - Null character (used to terminate strings)
